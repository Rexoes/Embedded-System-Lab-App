STM32F407VGT6 - 7 Segment Display Pinout (1 Digit);
---------------------------------------------------
MCU		7 Segment Display
PE0			A
PE1			B
PE2			C
PE3			D
PE4			E
PE5			F
PE6			G
PE10		     DIGIT_1


GPIO Configuration;
---------------------------------------------------
PE10 (DIGIT_1)			: GPIO OUTPUT, Max Output Speed : Very High
PE0 - PE6			: GPIO OUTPUT
